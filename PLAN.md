# Hiring Platform - Product Flow

## Overview

The platform serves two primary user roles:

- Job Seeker
- Employer / Recruiter

A user selects their role during onboarding. Some common modules (authentication, profile, notifications, settings, wallet, AI assistant) are shared, while dashboards and workflows differ based on the selected role.
